# First Day at University

My first day at university was a memorable experience, as I went there with my parents. Their presence gave me some comfort, but I still felt quite nervous because it was my very first day in a completely new environment. Everything seemed unfamiliar, from the campus to the people around me. When I arrived, some senior students guided us about our classes and departments. They helped me understand where to go and what to expect, which reduced my confusion to some extent. Even with their help, I still felt anxious, as I was stepping into a new phase of life where everything was different from school.

During the first two days, we had an orientation program. These sessions were very helpful, as the teachers introduced themselves and explained different aspects of university life. They encouraged us to stay confident, work hard, and make the most of our time at university. Their motivational words made me feel more comfortable and less nervous. I started to feel that I could adjust to this new environment with time and effort. The orientation also helped me understand the importance of discipline, participation, and consistency in studies.

Later, when I checked my timetable, I came to know that Sir Bilal would be teaching us theory. This made me curious, as I was about to begin learning new subjects related to my field. Although I was still a bit nervous, I also felt excited about starting my academic journey and learning new things step by step.
